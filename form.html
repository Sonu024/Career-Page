Job Description:

We are looking for a skilled and motivated Software Developer to join our IT team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for building, enhancing, and maintaining software solutions that are scalable, secure, and performance-driven. You will work alongside designers, developers, and product managers to deliver high-quality applications that meet business needs and user expectations.

Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, test, and deploy software applications and features
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams (designers, product managers, QA)
  • Write clean, efficient, and maintainable code
  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to the team’s technical growth
  • Troubleshoot, debug, and upgrade existing systems
  • Stay updated with the latest industry trends and emerging technologies
  • Ensure application security and data protection best practices
  • Manage software documentation and technical specifications

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field
  • 1–4 years of experience in software development
  • Solid understanding of object-oriented programming and software design patterns
  • Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git)
  • Familiarity with Agile or Scrum methodologies
  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment
